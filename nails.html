About me

Nails Nails Nails

I am not a very materialistic person, and don't know what I am doing when it comes to make-up. This is why everyone is always shocked that I always have manicured hands. Painting my nails is one of the most theraputic hobbies I have, and I also love painting other peoples, much to my boyfriends dismay (he's only been subjected to it once).

I haven't bought new nail varnish recently, but my collection has grown a lot over the years and I store it all in colour-coded rows. I delight in having someone else choose a colour for my nails and have occasionally branched out into nail art, especially for special occasions. In the past I have had themes nails for Christmas, Halloween and even Valentine's day.
